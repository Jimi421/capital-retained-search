About Capital Retained Search

Founded by Kathryn Hoover in Raleigh, North Carolina, Capital Retained Search is a boutique firm focused on retained executive and high-leverage IC roles. We combine thoughtful outreach with structured evaluations to deliver diverse, values-aligned leaders who create measurable impact.

Before launching Capital Retained Search, Kathryn held leadership roles in executive recruiting and has partnered with growth-stage companies and PE portfolio teams across revenue, operations, product, finance, and people functions. She serves her community through board service and is a graduate of UNC–Chapel Hill.

We operate with strict confidentiality and conflict checks on every engagement.

FAQ

How does retained search differ from contingent?

Retained engagements reserve dedicated time and deliver a managed process, market intel, and guaranteed delivery—ideal for confidential or high-impact roles.

What functions do you cover?

Revenue leadership, operations/GTM, product & technology, finance & people, and other corporate roles.

How quickly do you produce a slate?

We present a scored shortlist in week one or two, with typical close around six weeks.

Do you run confidential backfills?

Yes. We handle discreet outreach and reference checks with tight controls.
